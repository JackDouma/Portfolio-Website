Esports Stat Tracking Website

A summer project made to track and display data of teams and players in an organized manner. This website was made for an esports organization to help organize and promote their teams. Admins are able to add teams and assign players to teams; teams would then upload their schedule and submit scores, which would then automatically update the stats of the team and player pages. This website is fully made with PHP and SQL for the backend, with simple CSS and bootstrap for the frontend.

Android Grade Tracking App

The purpose of this application is to help students track how well they are doing in their courses. When you login, you can add a course, and in each course you can add any amount of grades as well as the overall weight of the grades. When you select a course, you will see a list of all your grades, and your current grade in that course. The app was made using Android Studio and Kotlin, and was uploaded to the google play store(since been taken down).

LinkedIn Recommender and Search System

This is a group of 3 final project in Big Data, that used a dataset of every job posting on LinkedIn, and allowed users to search and select jobs based on search queries. Based on the jobs they applied for, they would be recommended new jobs using collaborative filtering, previous search terms, and similar jobs they have applied for. This project uses HTML rendered dynamically through the Flask library in Python. Bootstrap was used for visuals.
